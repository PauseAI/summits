## AI Policy Structure

Australia's AI policy is distributed across multiple departments:
- **DISR**: Lead on national AI strategy, National AI Centre
- **DFAT**: International AI diplomacy, cyber/critical tech
- **DTA**: Government AI adoption, digital transformation
- **Home Affairs**: AI in critical infrastructure, national security
- **AGD**: AI legal/regulatory frameworks, human rights

No dedicated AI Safety Institute, but National AI Centre (under DISR) handles coordination.
