## AI Policy Structure

AI policy in the UK is led by DSIT, created in February 2023. Key components:

- **AI Policy Directorate**: Within DSIT, absorbed the former Office for Artificial Intelligence
- **AI Safety Institute (AISI)**: Established November 2023, world's first national AI safety institute
- **Incubator for AI (i.AI)**: Within Government Digital Service, develops AI tools for government
- **Cabinet Office AI team**: ai@digital.cabinet-office.gov.uk

The UK positions itself as a leader in "pro-innovation" AI governance while maintaining safety focus.
